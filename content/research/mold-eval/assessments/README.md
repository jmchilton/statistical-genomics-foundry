# Assessment scorecards

Per-subject scorecards written by `/assess-external-skill` and `/assess-mold`. Each grades one
subject across the four mold-eval rubrics (`../rubrics/`) as a **four-letter vector** — Operability ·
Assessability · Traceability · Refereeability — plus evidence, `[verify]` items, and a work-list.
The **grading criteria live in the rubrics**; these files are the **graded results**, and are why
the rubrics themselves stay abstract.

- `external-<skill-slug>.md` — an external skill's mirror feasibility ("could we build it?").
- `mold-<mold-slug>.md` — one of our Molds as it stands.
